About me
I was born in Italy, in 1992. Specifically in Treviso, a small town in the north-east of Italy. Currently, I live in Szczecin since October 2018, a town in the north-west of Poland, where I'm studying to be PhD in astrophysics. The group I am working in is very theoretical, but I prefer data and observation! In fact, I graduated in astronomy in Padova in the September of 2017, after a year in Stockholm with the Erasmus project, where I worked on my master thesis. Here, you can take a look at my CV.

I like to travel, quite a lot. I particularly fell in love with Portugal and large part of America (Mexico, USA, Chile).
